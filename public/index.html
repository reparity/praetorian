About Us

Rdein Laboratories is a medical research and development group dedicated to the study of Originium and Originium-based infections. We specialise in the environmental effects of Originium spread as well as working in tandem with pharmaceutical companies to produce a panacea for Infected across Terra.

Locations

We are headquartered in the Rhinelands, but we have research enclaves all around Terra. Of note are the enclaves in the South Pole, Victoria, Rokkenjima, and an attache with Rhodes Island.

Staff

Our staff consists of many of the brightest minds Terra has to offer. The expertise we have on board Rdein Lab ranges from mechatronic engineering to data analysis and even frontline medical care. Due to the often dangerous nature of our work, most of our field technicians operate with codenames to preserve anonymity.

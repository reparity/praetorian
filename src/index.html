Maifara Hotel

Maifara Hotel is located in the heart of Kuala Lumpur City Centre (KLCC) and offers the panoramic view of the Petronas Twin Towers, KLCC Park and the city's skyline. It offers direct covered access to the Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre and is within walking distance of Suria KLCC Shopping Mall, the Bukit Bintang shopping district and local tourist attractions, which are all surrounded by restaurants, bars, clubs and cafes. and released under the Creative Commons License.
